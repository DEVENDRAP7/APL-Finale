The Challenge
What breaks at 72,000 people

Every large event faces the same systemic failures — and they all compound each other at scale.

🚶‍♂️

Crowd Bottlenecks

Entry gates and concourses become dangerously congested with no real-time intelligence to redistribute flow.

⏳

Long Wait Times

Concession queues of 15–25 minutes with no dynamic staffing response or attendee guidance to alternatives.

📻

Siloed Staff Comms

Security, medics, ushers, and logistics operate on separate channels with no unified coordination layer.

🚨

Slow Emergency Response

Medical incidents and safety threats take minutes to escalate — time that costs lives in critical situations.

🗺️

No Personal Guidance

Attendees navigate 72,000-person venues alone — no smart assistant for seat finding, food orders, or accessibility.

🚗

Post-Game Chaos

Exit surges overwhelm parking lots and transport with no predictive management or dynamic routing.

The AI Agent Ecosystem

7 agents working in a 3-tier hierarchy: a Predictive forecaster, 3 Specialist agents on the operational frontline, and 3 Trade-off agents whose disagreements are resolved by the Master Orchestrator.

🔮 PREDICTIVE TIER
🔮
Surge Prediction Agent
gemini-2.5-pro · 5-min horizon · Confidence-rated

Projects zone density 5 minutes into the future from current state plus scenario trajectory. Surfaces ETAs ("Gate North will hit 85% in 3:20") so staff can pre-position before the surge actually lands.

💬
Attendee Experience Agent
gemini-3.1-flash-lite · Streaming · WebSocket

Handles every fan's personal query in natural language — seat finding, accessible routes, food ordering, lost & found. Streams responses in real time to the mobile app.

Emergency Response Agent
gemini-3.1-pro · Adaptive thinking · 2048 tokens

Safety-critical reasoning for medical incidents, security threats, fires, and evacuations. Uses gemini-3.1-pro because every emergency is a novel combination no ruleset can cover.

Vision Anomaly Agent
gemini-3.1-pro · Vision · anomaly > 0.7

Interprets flagged camera frames using Gemini Vision. YOLOv8 runs free on all 96 cameras every 5s. When anomaly_score exceeds 0.7, Gemini Vision determines exactly what's happening.

🛡️
Safety Agent
gemini-3-pro · Veto authority · weight 0.50

Owns crush-risk thresholds. Can veto any specialist decision when crowd density passes 96% or when crowd-flow vectors enter a danger envelope.

⚙️
Operations Agent
gemini-3.5-flash · weight 0.20

Argues for staff workload limits, shift rotation, and operational sustainability. Prevents over-deployment of security or medics already on long shifts — ensures the human side of the venue stays sharp.

staff_fatigue_scoreshift_rotation_checkdeployment_sustainability
⭐
Experience Agent
gemini-3.5-flash · weight 0.30

Represents the attendee point-of-view in trade-offs. Proposes "partial" or compromise options (one-way flow, staggered release) that preserve perceived service quality.

The top of the agent hierarchy. Fires only when specialist agents disagree on a decision. Weighs all stances (Safety 0.50, Experience 0.30, Operations 0.20) and publishes the binding resolution to the venue's action layer. Live demo of its reasoning is in the Multi-Agent Negotiation section above.

Architecture
How it works

Five layers from physical hardware to real-world action. The rule engine handles 80% instantly — AI agents handle the 20% rules can't.

1

Physical Sensors

284 IoT sensors + 96 CCTV cameras publish data every 2–5 seconds via MQTT and RTSP streams.

2

Ingestion Pipeline

Raw sensor data is normalised, threshold-gated, and enriched with zone metadata before entering the system.

3

Rule Engine

80% of decisions — crowd routing, queue alerts, staff dispatch, parking — are instant rule-based code. Zero API cost.

4

AI Agents

3 Gemini agents handle what rules can't: natural language chat, novel emergency reasoning, visual anomaly interpretation.

5

Real-World Action

PA broadcasts, digital signage, staff dispatch, mobile notifications, and emergency calls — all automated.
